    Immersive 3D Mobile Gaming for the Visually Impaired: This innovative mobile gaming solution earned national...

    This mobile game is a groundbreaking innovation tailored for visually impaired individuals, offering an inclusive gaming experience that transcends traditional visual constraints. The gameplay leverages cutting-edge 3D sound effects, creating an immersive environment that simulates real-world scenarios through spatial audio. Players can engage fully in the game by wearing a headset to hear and interact with the game world.

    The core gameplay mechanics are designed to be intuitive and accessible. Users control the gameplay by shaking their phones left or right, enhancing interactivity without requiring visual input. This innovative control system, combined with realistic soundscapes, allows players to navigate, react, and participate in the game seamlessly.

    Microsoft Imagine Cup National Finalists World Citizenship Category

    Innovative Expired Product Detection System: This solution was selected as a finalist in the Microsoft Imagine Cup...

    This groundbreaking solution was designed to eliminate the sale of expired products globally, with a particular focus on food and pharmaceuticals. Recognizing the harmful impact of expired products on public health, the system integrates a barcode with the expiration date. When a product is scanned at the Point-of-Sale (POS) system, it alerts the seller if the product is expired and prevents the sale from proceeding.

    Additionally, the system helps sellers efficiently manage inventory by tracking expiration dates, enabling timely removal of expired items. This innovative approach enhances consumer safety, streamlines inventory management, and ensures regulatory compliance.

    I led the team from ideation to development, and this solution was selected as a finalist in the Microsoft Imagine Cup National Finals, showcasing its potential for large-scale impact and innovation.
